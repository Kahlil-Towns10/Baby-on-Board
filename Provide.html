How to provide for your child

Finding a balance between work and your child

  • Don't get into the workaholic lifestyle where you barely have any time for you child
  • To have a good relationship with your child, ask them about moving to a new place for a job offer and try to convince them, don't just make the decision without them

    • Give them some space

  • This is mainly meant for teenagers as they are going through puberty and may want to spent more time with their friends than their parents
  • Respecting this decision can make them like you more
  • You can still support them, just don't try to be in all their business 24/7

    • Ask for their opinions

  • This is mainly meant for teenagers as they are becoming independent and will have to make decisions without you
  • Guiding them to choose the right choices in situations can have an impact on their opinions later in life

    • Be honest

  • If your child knows that you will be honest with them they will come to talk to you more
  • You may also build up trust with being honest
